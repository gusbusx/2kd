Fire Safety Standard

Don't Risk a Fine You Don't Need!

Businesses that operate commercial kitchens are legally required to maintain their exhaust hood and ventilation systems to reduce the risk of fire hazards. Over time, grease and combustible residue build up inside the hood, ductwork, and exhaust fan. If these systems are not cleaned regularly, the buildup can become extremely dangerous and significantly increase the risk of a kitchen fire. Failure to properly maintain these systems can also lead to serious consequences, including costly fines, insurance violations, failed inspections, or even temporary closure of the business by local authorities.

To ensure the highest level of safety and compliance, we strictly follow the cleaning and inspection standards established by the National Fire Protection Association (NFPA). Founded in 1896, the NFPA is the leading authority in fire prevention and safety codes used throughout the United States and around the world. Their guidelines—particularly NFPA 96, which focuses on ventilation control and fire protection for commercial cooking operations—are the industry standard used by fire inspectors, insurance companies, and regulatory agencies.

By following NFPA standards, we ensure that every cleaning is performed thoroughly and according to the proper safety procedures. Our goal is not only to keep your kitchen compliant with fire codes but also to protect your staff, your customers, and your business from the serious dangers associated with grease fires. Maintaining a clean exhaust system helps improve ventilation efficiency, reduce fire risk, and keep your kitchen operating safely and professionally.

Certified Through MFS Trade School

At 2KD Kitchen Hood Cleaning, our technicians are professionally trained and certified through MFS Trade School (MFS), one of the most recognized institutions in the country for commercial kitchen exhaust hood cleaning certification. This specialized training ensures that our team understands the proper techniques, safety procedures, and industry standards required to clean and maintain commercial kitchen exhaust systems correctly.

Through this certification, our technicians are educated on the full cleaning process from removing grease buildup inside the hood canopy to thoroughly cleaning the ductwork and exhaust fan where dangerous grease accumulation often occurs. This training also emphasizes compliance with fire safety standards, proper chemical use, equipment handling, and the correct documentation required during professional hood cleaning services.

Being certified through MFS Trade School means our team is trained to perform each job safely, thoroughly, and in accordance with professional industry guidelines. We take pride in maintaining a high level of professionalism and attention to detail on every service. Our goal is not only to leave your system visibly clean, but to help ensure your kitchen remains safe, compliant with regulations, and operating at its best.

At 2KD Kitchen Hood Cleaning, certification is more than just a credential—it reflects our commitment to providing reliable, high-quality service that restaurant owners, kitchen managers, and inspectors can trust.
